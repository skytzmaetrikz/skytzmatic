The Archive

Three decades of source material: origins, WMD era, military leadership, vault years, and the current expansion phase.

1996–1998
Origins — Started rapping as a teenager in Long Beach. First blackout freestyle session with Dred and Tek22 in Compton. Selected for Spread the News super lyricist group. Grabbed a dictionary, karaoke machine, and maxi-singles. The journey began.
1998
Verbal Warfare — First studio-recorded song. Earliest documented studio artifact in the Skytzmatic archive. The voice was already there.
2000–2003
WMD Era — Underground group catalog. Approximately 90 songs in the vault. Original Titanium Turnbuckle lineage begins here. Voice fully crystallized on Microphone Dreams recording.
2003–2014
Catalog Expansion — 11 releases. Microphone Dreams: The Prequel through Backpack Gangsta Rap Vol. 4. Feat. Ruste Juxx on S.F.T.C. USAF service. Single father. Responsibilities handled.
2014–2026
Vault Years / Air Force Era — Stepped away to raise his son. That son is now serving in the Air Force. Catalog preserved. Marcus Masters 2007–2017 archive secured. Nothing lost.
May 17, 2026
Reactivation — Full catalog uploaded to DistroKid. skytzmatic.com secured. All platforms rebuilt. The mission resumes.
June 1, 2026
First Strike — Move Out The... released. 4 playlist placements. 21 curators hit in one day. ReverbNation Top 10. The machine activates.
June 15, 2026
Wave 2 — World / Endless + Start Up / Merciless. All DSPs. Editorial pitched. Canvas live. Groover campaign launches.
