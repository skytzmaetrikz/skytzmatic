About
Skytzmatic is Supreme Knowledge Yielding Truth Zealously Manifesting Asymmetric Tactical Infinite Consciousness.

USAF Vet. CSULB Alum. California Conservation Corps Alum. A Long Beach, California emcee who doesn't ask for permission. Built in the boom bap tradition — raw, precise, and built to outlast everything around it. He doesn't chase rhymes, he dismantles emcees. A rapper and producer who brings both sides of the craft like a weapon system.

He spent 2003 to 2014 constructing a catalog that documents one thing — the complete evolution of a weapon. Bar by bar. Record by record. No shortcuts. No compromises. His approach is asymmetric warfare on wax. Unconventional tactics. Precise execution.

As a single father who raised his son, Skytzmatic has returned with nothing left to prove and everything left to say. The responsibilities that pulled him away are handled. Now the mic gets what it's always been owed.

Committed to the art of lyrical swordplay — sharp, precise, and built to eviscerate everything counterfeit in the game — he bleeds lyricism the way others breathe oxygen. Without it, there is no life. Backpack in one hand. Pen in the other. The catalog was just the opening statement.
Boom bap. No hooks. No features. No apologies.
