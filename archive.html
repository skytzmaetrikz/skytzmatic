Operational History

Timeline

2026 — Present
Mission Resumed Era
Full reactivation May 17, 2026. Infrastructure rebuilt from zero. BGR Vol. 5 Wave 1 deployed June 15. Dilemaradio editorial placement June 16 — 45 SPINs / 97.8%. 16+ countries reached organically. Archive recovery initiated. Loyalty & Legacy.
2009 – 2014
Long Beach Era — BGR Catalog
Post-military return. Loyalty Over Money Records fully operational. BGR Vol. 1–4 recorded and released. Live shows documented — Whiskey A-Go-Go (Sunset Blvd, LA), King Ryan Events. Professional photography by JD Starling, Rick Garcia / SHGfoto. Press interviews on red carpet. LOM merch — physical CDs, shirts, brand collateral. Cashmere Agency relationship. International feature — Demi-D (Korea). LOM roster — C-4 (rest in peace).
2007 – 2008
USAF Era — Spain Recording
ASCAP registered — writer and publisher. Loyalty Over Money Records founded. Microphone Dreams LP recorded at Santa Studios, Rota, Cadiz, Spain — on active duty. Featuring Ruste Juxx (Duck Down Records) and Ced-G (Prophetic Poets). 19 works registered. Physical CD. First weekend back from deployment documented.
2003 – 2006
USAF Era — Active Duty / Early Catalog
United States Air Force — active duty — deployed Iraq 2003. C-130 Hercules, MH-53 Pave Low. Multiple albums recorded and released during service. Microphone Dreams: The Prequel, Life In The Fast Lane, Microphone Dreams EP, S.F.T.C. Prophetic Poets — Life As We See It // Chapter One — 20 tracks.
1996 – 2002
Origins — Long Beach
Started 1996, Long Beach, CA. All four elements: breaking, tagging, battle rap, recording. Name given in a freestyle session. TDK cassette demos — Universal Kompound. Prophetic Poets formed. Underground circuit. The foundation was laid before the first studio was booked.
