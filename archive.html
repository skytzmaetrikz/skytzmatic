Operational History

Timeline

2026 — Present
Mission Resumed Era
Full reactivation May 17, 2026. BGR Vol. 5 Wave 1 deployed June 15. Dilemaradio editorial placement June 16 — 45 SPINs / 97.8%. 16+ countries reached organically. Archive recovery initiated. Loyalty & Legacy.
2009 – 2014
Long Beach Era — BGR Catalog
Post-military return. Loyalty Over Money fully operational. BGR Vol. 1–4 recorded and released. The Year of Enlightenment produced, recorded and released. Live shows — Headline Whiskey-A-Go-Go (Sunset Blvd, LA), King Ryan Events. Professional photography — JD Starling, Rick Garcia / SHGfoto. Press interviews on red carpet. LOM merch — physical CDs, shirts. Joint merch venture with 2nd Hustle Clothing. Cashmere Agency relationships. International feature — Demi-D (Korean Rapper). LOM roster — C-4 (rest in peace).
2004 – 2009
USAF Era — Spain Recordings
Prophetic Poets formed with Ced-G, Son of Soul, and Jay Flow. Recorded Prophetic Poets — Life As We See It // Chapter One: The Movement, Knights of the Old Republic Mixtape, Life In The Fast Lane LP, Microphone Dreams EP, Smashing for the Cause (S.F.T.C.) EP. ASCAP registered — writer and publisher. Loyalty Over Money Records founded. Microphone Dreams LP recorded at Urban Doctor Studio, Rota, Spain. Featuring Ruste Juxx (Duck Down Records) and Ced-G (Prophetic Poets). 19 works registered. Physical CD.
2000 – 2004
USAF Era — McChord AFB — Early Catalog
WMD formed with Stepchild — USAF Punishers. Over 90 unreleased songs and freestyles. 2001 — home studio recordings with Magix Music Maker 2000, software purchased with scholarship money awarded from completion of the California Conservation Corps program. Released Microphone Dreams: The Prequel. Physical CD.
1998 – 2000
California Conservation Corps Era
Northern California. Recorded first professional song, Verbal Warfare, with K.T. Productions in Santa Rosa, CA. Featured on several Bay Area compilation projects.
1997 – 1998
Universal Kompound
Members — T.Berry, Weatherman, Griff Skillz, Mz. Divine. TDK cassette recordings. Underground circuit.
1994 – 1996
Origins — Long Beach
All four elements: breaking, tagging, battle rap, recording. Name given in a freestyle session.
