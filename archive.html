Operational History

Timeline

2026 — Present
Comeback Era — Reactivated
Full reactivation May 17, 2026. Infrastructure rebuilt from zero. BGR Vol. 5 Wave 1 deployed June 15. Dilemaradio editorial placement June 16. 16+ countries. Archive recovery initiated. The Pack being built.
2003 – 2014
Long Beach Era — Catalog Built
Post-military return. BGR series launched. Vol. 1 through Vol. 4 recorded and released. West Coast underground circuit. Independent — no label, no industry support. Catalog spans 11+ releases.
2007 – 2008
USAF Era — Spain Recording
ASCAP registered as writer and publisher. Loyalty Over Money Records founded. Microphone Dreams LP recorded at Santa Studios, Rota, Cadiz, Spain. 19 works registered. Physical CD release. Featuring Ruste Juxx (Duck Down Records).
2003 – 2006
USAF Era — Early Catalog
First commercial recordings. Active duty, United States Air Force. Multiple albums recorded and released during service. Smashing for the Cause, Life In The Fast Lane, Microphone Dreams EP, The Prequel.
1996 – 2002
Origins — Long Beach
Started 1996, Long Beach, CA. All four elements: breaking, tagging, battle rap, recording. Name given in a freestyle session. TDK cassette demos. Underground circuit. Musicians Institute. California Conservation Corps. CSULB. The foundation was laid before the first studio was booked.
